POLi Payments for WooCommerce enables POLi payments on the WooCommerce checkout. Enable your customers to pay directly from their bank account without having to using a credit card.

== Description ==
The POLi Payments for WooCommerce plugin allows you to accept bank account payments directly from your customers quickly and easily. In order to configure the plugin, store owners will need to first [sign up for a POLi account](https://www.polipay.co.nz/sell-with-poli/get-poli/?SC=woocommerce2)

POLi is a debit payment system which allows your customers to make immediate payments directly from their bank account. There is no credit or debit card required to make a payment using POLi, only a bank account with any of the supported banks within the region. 


POLi is available for Merchants with a bank account in:

* New Zealand

**Features**

* Low transaction fees
* Multi-currency support
* Control reconciliation data
* Smooth checkout flow that handles all the security for you
* Accept debit bank payments
* Branding on payment screen
* Real-time reporting from the POLi Console
* Friendly support team

== Installation ==

**Automatic Installation from the Wordpress Dashboard**

1. From your dashboard navigate to “Plugins -> Add New”
1. Search for ‘POLi Payments for WooCommerce’;
1. Click ‘Install Now’;
1. Activate the plugin;
1. Configure your POLi account details into the plugin

**Manual Installation**

The manual installation method involves downloading our plugin and uploading it to your web server via your favourite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here.](https://wordpress.org/support/article/managing-plugins/)

You can request a plugin for older versions of WooCommerce [from our support team.](https://www.polipay.co.nz/support/)

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is POLi? = 

POLi in an online payment system that allows customers to make payments direct from their bank account.

= Why Choose POLi? =

POLi is a great alternative for people without credit cards, or for those who have them but would prefer not to use them to shop online or pay a bill online. 
Merchants who offer POLi on their checkout sell more by addressing a wider audience, substantially lower their payment costs and improve payment reconciliation.


= Who Can Pay using POLi? =

Any customer who has access to the “One-off Payment” functionality offered by a supported online bank. To see a full list of our supported banks, [click here](https://www.polipayments.com/banks)

= What currencies does POLi support? = 

New Zealand Dollars (NZD).  Customers need to have a bank account within New Zealand in order to make a payment.

= Does POLi accept credit cards? =

No, POLi is a debit payment option that allows customers to pay you direct from their bank account.

= How do I sign up? = 

[Click Here](http://www.polipay.co.nz/start) to make an application for a POLi account.

= Is POLi Secure? = 

Yes. [Click here](https://www.polipay.co.nz/sell-with-poli/merchant-security/) to see details of POLi security.

= What version of Woocommerce do I need? =

The latest version of the POLi Woocommerce plugin supports versions 3.0.1 and above.  If you have an older version of Woocommerce you can [request a plugin](https://www.polipay.co.nz/developers).
